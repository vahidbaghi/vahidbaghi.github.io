Role:  Course Developer

The course developer develops training material to teach users how to use the product. This includes creating slides, student notes, examples, tutorials, and so on to enhance users' understanding of the product.

Staffing

A course developer should have experience and/or training in course development.  He/she requires a good understanding of the product, and preferably a good understanding of the users' needs.
