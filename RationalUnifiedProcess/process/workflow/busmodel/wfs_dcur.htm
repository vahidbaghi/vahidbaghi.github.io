Workflow Detail:  Describe Current Business

Topics

Purpose

How to Staff

Work Guidelines


Purpose

The purpose of this workflow detail is to:

  • Understand the current target-organization's processes and structure. 
  • Based on this understanding, refine the goals of the business-modeling effort.  

The purpose is not to describe the current target-organization in detailonly enough of it to be able to prioritize which parts of the target-organization to focus on for the remainder of the project. 

How to Staff

Your business-modeling team, all acting as Business-Process Analysts, invites the stakeholders representatives. This extended business-modeling team needs to cover good business domain knowledge and know how current systems are used to automate the business. The core team members need to have good facilitation skills.

Work Guidelines

Conduct a workshop, where the goal is to understand and outline what the business use cases and business actors are in the current organization. The following sample techniques can be applied to help you collect correct and relevant information:

  • Brain storming and idea reduction
  • Use-case workshops
  • Storyboarding
  • Role playing
