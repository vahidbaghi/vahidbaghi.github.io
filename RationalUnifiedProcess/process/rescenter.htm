If you have Internet access and are a Rational customer, you can access the RUP Center at the Rational Developer Network, which provides you with specific portions of the RUP, and information about how to apply and extend it.  You'll also find information relevant to RUP that's not included in the product, such as:

  • Whitepapers 

  • Technical Articles 

  • Documentation and Technical Notes

  • Recommended Links to other relevant sites

The Rational Developer Network provides an online development community discussion forum, education about software development, Rational tools training, and guidance on development methodologies.
